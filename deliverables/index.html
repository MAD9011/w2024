# MAD9011 Deliverables

Every week there will be work for you to complete inside or outside of class. Some exercises will be larger than others. There will be 6 exercises (20%), midterm (25%), Final project (40%), and 3 quizzes (15%).

# Exercises 20%


Exercise 1 — Select & Mask

Due: Tuesday January 16th @9:00pm

In this exercise, you'll enhance your skills in non-destructive masking and background replacement, progressing to create a unified composition by blending multiple images into a visually and narratively compelling scene.

View Details

Exercise 2 — Photo Retouching

Due: Tuesday January 23rd @9:00pm

In this exercise, you'll apply the retouching techniques learned in class, emphasizing the importance of using non-destructive editing methods throughout the task.

View Details

Exercise 3 — Logo Recreation

Due: Tuesday January 30th @9:00pm

In this exercise, you'll learn how to use basic shapes for logo creation using simple shapes and then utilizing pathfinder tools and anchor point manipulation

View Details

Exercise 4 — Button Design

Due: Tuesday February 6th @9:00pm

In this exercise, you'll delve into UI and user-centered design principles to create a set of buttons, strategically employing color theory and gradient tools to ensure they are visually appealing and enhance user interaction.

View Details

Exercise 5 — Creating Icons

Due: Tuesday February 13th @9:00pm

In this exercise, you'll design a cohesive set of icons tailored for use in a mobile app, ensuring they harmonize in style and functionality.

View Details

Exercise 6 — Animated SVG

Due: Sunday February 18th @11:59pm

In this exercise, you'll create an engaging application loader using animated SVG in Adobe Illustrator, focusing on design aesthetics, clean export setup, and CSS-based animation styling.

View Details

# Midterm 25%


Mobile Design Assets

Due: Sunday February 25th @11:59pm

For your midterm project, you'll craft a comprehensive suite of mobile design assets, encompassing everything from optimized images to bespoke icons, leveraging the capabilities of both Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator.

View Details

# Final Project 40%


Part 1: Low-fidelity Wireframes

Due: Tuesday March 12th @7:00pm

In part 1 of your final project, you'll develop a series of low-fidelity wireframes, laying the foundational design blueprint for a mobile application.

View Details

Part 2: Design System

Due: Tuesday March 26th @7:00pm

In part 2 of your final project, you'll develop a mini design system, creating guidelines and standards for elements such as typography, color palettes, and UI components to ensure consistency and coherence in your design approach.

View Details

Part 3: High-fidelity Wireframes

Due: Tuesday April 9th @7:00pm

In part 3 of your final project, you'll elevate your mid-fidelity wireframes to high-fidelity by meticulously applying the principles and elements from your design system, ensuring a polished and detailed representation of your final design concept.

View Details

Part 4: Interactive Visual Prototype

Due: Tuesday April 16th @7:00pm

In part 4 of your final project, you'll bring your high-fidelity wireframes to life by integrating interactivity and animations, preparing them for a comprehensive user testing experience that closely simulates the final product.

View Details

Part 5: Presentations

Due: Tuesday April 16th @9:00pm

In part 5 of your final project, you'll showcase the culmination of your efforts from parts 1 to 4, including a comprehensive presentation and a live demonstration of your interactive prototype, highlighting the journey from initial concepts to the final interactive design.

View Details
